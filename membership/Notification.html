    ACM SIGSPATIAL China Membership Registration Notification


    Dear Colleagues,


    ACM SIGSPATIAL China Chapter has been in existence for a decade since it was founded in 2009. Recently it has completed a new re-election. To strengthen linkages with ACM, we invite you to register the ACM SIGSPATIAL China Chapter membership sincerely. Please note that, before you become the membership of our organization, firstly you need to register as an ACM SIGSPATIAL member. We invite you to fill out the form by clicking the link or scan the QR code at the end of the article to record the existing membership and apply new membership.


  • ACM SIGSPATIAL China Registration:

    • Please fill out the application form by clicking the link or scan the QR code at the end of the article.


  • ACM SIGSPATIAL Registration:

    • To encourage you to register the membership actively, we sponsor 20 free ACM SIGSPATIAL registration places (about 15$). When you register for an ACM SIGSPATIAL membership, please go to the register link to register with $15. Alternatively, you can apply for the free chance with your reason in the form by clicking the link or scan the QR code at the end of the article. We will reply to you by email after verification.


    In detail, you can fill out the form refer to the following four cases:

  • If you are already an ACM SIGSPATIAL member, as well as an ACM SIGSPATIAL China Chapter member, you only need to confirm the existing membership.
  • If you are already an ACM SIGSPATIAL member, but not an ACM SIGSPATIAL China Chapter member yet, we invite you to register for the latter cordially.
  • If you have been involved in the ACM SIGSPATIAL China Chapter, but not an ACM SIGSPATIAL member yet, we invite you to register for the ACM SIGSPATIAL member.
  • If you have never registered for both, we invite you to complete the registration of ACM SIGSPATIAL and ACM SIGSPATIAL China Chapter step by step.
